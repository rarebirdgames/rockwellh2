The Pitch

Sinfeld: Evil Resident is a surreal horror-comedy inspired by Silent Hill 4, No More Heroes, and Metal Gear Solid.

You play as Donathan, an orphan comedian seeking refuge in the abandoned apartment of his comedic idol, Gary Sinfeld. But Gary is asking for your help in resurrecting him and his friends from the Death Zone.

"Inspired by my own experiences in the film and television industry, I wanted to tell a story about how our addiction to IP will lead us to our own downfall."
