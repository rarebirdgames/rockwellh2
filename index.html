DESCRIPTION

Sinfeld: Evil Resident is a surreal, narrative-driven horror-comedy experience inspired by the psychological dread of survival horror classics, but with the absurdity of a sitcom gone wrong.

Players become Donathan, an orphan comedian trapped in the eerie apartment of his comedic idol. This once-normal apartment quickly reveals itself as a twisted maze of secret compartments and unsettling relics. Enter the "Death Zone": crawl through disturbing rifts into surreal nightmare worlds confronting manifestations of personal demons.
